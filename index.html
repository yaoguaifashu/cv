About

2021疫情后的新纪元我回到了上海。因为从小梦想成为一位世界公民，喜欢多元价值观，所以选择远离自己的故乡，旅居日本（东京，千叶，仙台，筑波，横滨）美国（西雅图） 欧洲（哥本哈根) 15年，并且一直在从事跨学科融合学科的研究，希望可以突破自然人文学科的分类，在理解自然理解社会的同时，成为富有人文精神的学者/创造者/终身学习的实践者。崇尚理性喜欢逻辑分析爱交朋友（尤其奇葩类）。对于可以推动社会变好的活动，也愿意积极参与。


Research focus wise, as an aspiring scientist & engineer with more than a decade's practical experience, Jing focus her research in Computing nature {Computational Physics, Chemistry and Material}, Computing human behaviour{Gaming data machine learning} and Numerical Simulation (C/C++,Python Java, R), while at the same time involved her research in the field of Advanced Material design, Molecular Thermodynamics, Fluid Dynamics, High Temperature Super Conductor, Kinetics of Chemical Reaction. She has published 14+ academic papers on Cryogenics et al. Jing also judged at the President & Dean Award in Chiba University in 2014.

Career focus wise, Jing specializes in fundamental & application research, education as well as creating bridges between government, industry and university - In recent years she has been working closely with universities as an Assitant Professor, and has been working as a consultant for both industry R & D department and venture capital sector in China and Japan.

Jing now opens for opportunities where she can help accelerate the transformation from fundamental research to commercial applications.
